Web Design Final Project Pitch

- faye zhang

mission statement

A well written mission statement of what you hope to accomplish with the project. This should be at least 200 words and include why you chose this specific site. Proposal should be written as if we (the class) were the client.

outline

An outline of what in the current site works and what doesn't work. Screen grabs can be used to demonstrate.

overview

An overview of what elements you want to use in the project and why. Examples would be a photo gallery, video/media page, slider, etc. The should be specific examples with demonstrations. Look to have at least four.

mockups

Two to three examples (or Mock-ups) to choose from. Mock-ups do not need to be functioning, but should give a good idea of what your home page will look like.
